About Us

GreenHarvest Spaces Africa is a youth-led initiative focused on climate action, environmental conservation, and sustainable food systems. We work with local communities to bridge information gaps through education, innovation, and action.

Our Focus Areas

  • 🌍 Climate Action (SDG 13)
  • 🌽 Food Security (SDG 2)
  • 📚 Environmental Education (SDG 4)
  • 🌳 Tree Planting & Ecosystem Restoration

Our Work

We engage teens, farmers, and communities through capacity building, participatory tree planting, and sustainable agriculture practices to build climate resilience. images of our work in action can be found on our social media channels and in our project reports.

Our Impact

Our initiatives have led to the restoration of over 5 hectares of degraded land, the empowerment of more than 200 students youth people, and inspired the improvement of food security and food systems throgh knowledge tranfer and capacity building. We are proud to contribute to the global effort to combat climate change and promote sustainable development in Africa.
